Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) is a Fortune 500 company and the largest private sector corporation in India. Founded by Dhirubhai Ambani in 1966, RIL has grown into a global conglomerate with operations spanning energy, petrochemicals, natural gas, retail, telecommunications, mass media, and textiles.

RIL is the parent company of Jio Platforms, Reliance Retail, and Reliance Jio — India's largest telecom network with 450M+ subscribers. With over 2,36,000 employees worldwide and a market capitalization exceeding ₹16 lakh crore, working with Reliance adds exceptional value to your professional career.
